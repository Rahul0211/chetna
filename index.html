What makes Chetna different from other fitness programmes? +
Chetna is run by a physician — not a personal trainer or nutritionist alone. Every programme begins with a medical assessment and comprehensive blood panel. Your diet, exercise, and lifestyle plan are derived from clinical data, not generic templates. This is metabolic fitness, not guesswork.
Do I need to have a medical condition to join? +
No. Chetna is designed for anyone who wants to optimise their health — whether you have a metabolic condition like Type 2 diabetes or hypertension, or simply want to lose weight, build endurance, and feel better. The clinical approach benefits everyone.
What does a typical week look like in the programme? +
Each batch runs for 3 months. Sessions run daily at [SESSION TIMINGS]. Information sessions and clinical check-ins are conducted at our clinic in Kopar Khairane. Exercise, physiotherapy, and activity sessions are held at different outdoor locations in nature. Each week includes a blend of metabolic conditioning (Zumba-style), yoga and core flexibility, and aerobic walk-run training, plus weekly check-ins and personalised nutrition guidance.
Are the sessions in-person or online? +
Chetna operates as an in-person programme from our centre in Kopar Khairane, Navi Mumbai. The medical consultations and group sessions are conducted face-to-face to ensure proper supervision and monitoring.
What results can I realistically expect? +
Results vary by individual baseline and adherence. What we can promise is that your progress is tracked using objective biomarkers — not just the scale. Batch 01 participants saw measurable improvements in metabolic markers, body composition, and energy levels. Many reported significant reduction in medication requirements for lifestyle diseases.
